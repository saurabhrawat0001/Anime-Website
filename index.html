MONSTER




As a doctor, Tenma's mission is save lives. But what if the boy he saved grows up to be a murderous psychopath? Should the giver of life take away a life? This series takes place in Germany and in the Czech Republic, encompassing the Fall of Berlin Wall, Czech Secret Police and Criminal Psychology of the late 20th century Johan Liebert: You will his story, but never will you know his name.





NAOKI URASAWA





AUTHOR OF MONSTER ANIME





Monster (a.k.a Naoki Urasawa's Monster ) is a seinen manga series written and illustrated by Naoki Urasawa, published by Shogakukan in Big Comic Original from December 5, 1994 to Dec 20, 2001, containing 162 chapters in 18 tankobon volumes. Urasawa later wrote and illustrated the novel Another Monster, a story detailing the events of the manga from an investigative reporter's point of view, which was published by Shogakukan in 2002.
